Zen Golf

Core philosophy

Purpose

Golf pits one against oneself so consistently and thus makes this a unique and divine sport. With consistent application anybody can become good at this sport though it is very challenging. Building solid fundamentals is the first step on your golfing journey and as the great Seve Ballesteros said "we spend hours on the fundamentals of the game because they are fundamental".

Holistic Improvement

The core premise of becoming good at golf is relentless application that ensures improvement. Improvement includes a key component namely temperament and the mental game plays a huge role in performance. All aspects related to this needs to be worked upon in a holistic manner.

Short Game

The key to building a good game quickly is to have a sharp short game. Having a great short game can relieve pressure on tee and approach shots. One doesn't need to worry about missing fairways and greens.

Correct Technique

Learn to Practise and Play with Purpose. Build well defined physical/mental routines to execute during a round of golf.

Keeping things simple

A key lesson in golf is to keep things simple. All the training has to be left aside and one has to switch to a trusting mode. One has to let things flow and that leads to peak performance

Building sound technique

Golf like life is all about building and maintain momentum. One has to learn to ease oneself into a round and build momentum. There is a lot of discipline needed to do this consistently.
Through the following programs, one can learn how to practice, improve and strategize. An essential component of this program is having someone to constantly bounce ideas and assess progress. Prospective students can constantly interact with the coach through WhatsApp, email and other platforms. Check out the programs in detail below.

Discovery Through Golf

  • A unique program designed specifically for self discovery and improvement
  • Golf is used as a tool and if one has an open mind then the possibilities are limitless
  • Ideal for folks who have never touched a golf club and are curious to know about golf and themselves!

Standard Program

  • 15 sessions ( home/range) plus 18 hole playing lesson on the course
  • Develop solid fundamentals
  • Focus on hand body drills and learn how to practice at home
  • Guidance with equipment
  • Enjoy, improve and preempt injuries

Swing/Short/ Mental Game

  • Work on all aspects of your short game, swing or the mental game
  • Focus on developing mental routines to help you on the course
  • 5 sessions focusing on different aspects of the short game/swing followed by 18 holes on the course
  • Learn course management, build your temperament and play HO HUM golf - The key to becoming a Scorer

Customized

  • Learn how to play HO HUM golf, strategy, course management
  • Work on tweaking your shortcomings/special shots/ other issues that hamper performance
  • Work out totally customized programs
  • Option to have a couple of playing lessons in Thailand ( group of 2 ONLY)

About Me

Vasuki Seshadri

I am a self taught golfer who picked up the golf club for the first time when I was 36, an age that many would consider too late; 4 years on I was playing to scratch and doing things on a golf course that should have taken me much longer. My accolades have not been extraordinary, however over the years I did achieve something quite extraordinary as well - I lived up to my full potential, and became the best possible golfer that I could have been; in more ways than one this thoroughly validates my methodology. Right from the beginning, I have rarely played more than a round a week but have consistently trained at home! As a coach, this is the same journey I bring to the lives of the people I work with - to bow down to this great game, be the best possible player you can be and enjoy the process more than the final number. Over the years, I still am and will always remain a very keen student of this divine sport.
