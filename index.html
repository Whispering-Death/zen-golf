Yolf

Core philosophy


Purpose

Yolf is an integrated approach to golf driven by the deeper precepts of yoga and meditation. At the core of peak perfromance in competition, is the ability to centered for the shot to be played. The yolf methodology is geared totally to this aspect of the game.

Swing

Each individual has to build and find their own swing. Tuning the body via yoga helps in longetivity and pre-empting injury both of which are critical to any golfer. Any injury cuts short a lovely journey and playing with fear of aggravating the injury hurting oneslef more kills the joy of playing this sport. Yolf is the perfect answer for anybody with an open mind to holistic improvement.

Short Game

The key to building a good game quickly is to have a sharp short game. Having a great short game can relieve pressure on tee and approach shots. Golf really is about converting 3 shots to 2. Golf is best learnt from Green to Tee (Harvey Penick).

Performance

Learning to Practice with a high degree of awareness is the key to constant improvement. This is termed as Application and the key to great golf is Relentless Application. There are really two games at play - one on the course which is the obvious one and the other one which is so much tougher that happens between the ears of the golfer. Yolf is a path to mastering both.

Building a good game

Golf like life is all about building and maintaining momentum. One has to learn to ease oneself into a round and build momentum. There is a lot of discipline needed to do this consistently. It's not easy to keep things simple on the golf course. All the training has to be left aside and one has to switch to a trusting mode-let things flow and experience the zone.
Through the following programs, one can learn how to practice, improve and strategize. A unique feature of this engagement is having someone to constantly bounce ideas and assess progress 24/7.
