Zen Golf

Core philosophy


Purpose

Golf pits one against oneself so consistently thus making it a very unique sport. Building sound fundamentals is absolutely necessary and as the legend Seve Ballesteros said “we spend hours on the fundamentals of the game because they are fundamental”.

Swing

Each individual has to build and find their own swing. This really means that while the fundamentals form the framework, one has to retain individuality.

Short Game

The key to building a good game quickly is to have a sharp short game. Having a great short game can relieve pressure on tee and approach shots. Golf really is about converting 3 shots to 2. Golf is best learnt from Green to Tee (Harvey Penick).

Performance

Learn to Practice and Play with a Purpose. Training and Playing are two entirely different aspects of this game and learning to PLAY on a golf course is easier said than done!

Building a good game

Golf like life is all about building and maintaining momentum. One has to learn to ease oneself into a round and build momentum. There is a lot of discipline needed to do this consistently. It's not easy to keep things simple on the golf course. All the training has to be left aside and one has to switch to a trusting mode-let things flow and experience the zone.
Through the following programs, one can learn how to practice, improve and strategize. An essential component of this program is having someone to constantly bounce ideas and assess progress. Prospective students can constantly interact with the coach through WhatsApp, email and other platforms. Check out the programs in detail below.

Standard Program

  • 15 sessions ( home/range) plus an 18 hole playing lesson on the course
  • Develop solid fundamentals
  • Focus on hand/body drills and learn how to practice at home
  • Guidance with equipment
  • Enjoy, improve and preempt injury

Customized

  • Learn how to play HO HUM golf, strategy, course management
  • Work on tweaking your shortcomings/special shots/ other issues that hamper performance
  • Work out totally customized programs
  • Learn at your home - privacy, convenience and noise free
  • Option to have a couple of playing lessons in Thailand ( group of 2 ONLY)

Discovery Through Golf

  • A unique program designed specifically for self discovery and improvement
  • Golf is used as a tool and if one has an open mind then the possibilities are limitless
  • Ideal for folks who have never touched a golf club and are curious to know about golf and themselves!

About Me

My accolades have not been extraordinary, however over the years I did achieve something quite extraordinary as well - I lived up to my full potential, and became the best possible golfer that I could have been; in more ways than one this thoroughly validates my methodology.
